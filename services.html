Optical Fiber

The optical fiber or optical fiber is a piece of glass or polymeric materials capable of transmitting light. Such a filament can have variable diameters, depending on the application, ranging from tiny diameters, on the order of micrometers (thinner than a strand of hair) to several millimeters.

The concept of optical fiber consists of using light to transport digital information through a medium made of small glass threads, where each one determines a path for the light rays that carry the signal.

VPN

A virtual private network is a mechanism for creating a secure connection between a computing device and a computer network, or between two networks, using an insecure communication medium such as the public Internet.
