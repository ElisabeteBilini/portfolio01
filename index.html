About Us

The company JGA Telecom has been operating in the telecommunication area since 2020, providing services in structured cabling, fiber optic fusion, optical cable launch. Our technicians have more than 10 years of experience in the information technology segment, generating reliability and security for our customers. We always seek the best service to our customers, before, during and after negotiations, always performing high quality services, security, honesty and compliance with deadlines.

Our mission

Aggregate knowledge aimed at continuous improvement in internal and external processes, in the quality of services and in the relationship with the market. Always offering high quality solutions, security, honesty and commitment to deadlines.

Vision

To be a reference in the quality of assistance in Information Technology.

Values

  • Efficient and shared management;
  • Commitment to growth and results;
  • Service and assistance focused on customer safety;
  • Team spirit, professional and institutional ethics;
